Welcome to Your Professional ENT Specialist

I am an otorhinolaryngologist, graduated from the University of Medicine in Tirana.

I perform aesthetic surgeries, including rhinoplasty, blepharoplasty, and otoplasty, as well as a wide range of procedures in the field of ENT, including tonsillectomy, adenoidectomy, and septoplasty.

Surgeries

Rhinoplasty

Rhinoplasty, commonly known as nose reshaping, refines the shape and structure of the nose for improved facial balance and, when needed, better breathing. Whether performed for aesthetic reasons, functional concerns, or both, Dr. Nikolla tailors each procedure to the unique features and goals of every patient.

Otoplasty

Otoplasty corrects the shape, size, or position of the ears, creating a more symmetrical and natural appearance. This procedure is often chosen to reduce prominent ears or reshape ear cartilage, restoring harmony to facial proportions while maintaining a natural look.

Blepharoplasty

Blepharoplasty revitalizes the delicate eye area by removing or repositioning excess skin, fat, and muscle from the upper and lower eyelids. This procedure brightens and refreshes your appearance, revealing eyes that look naturally youthful, alert, and full of life.
