Blepharoplasty – Eyelid Surgery for a Refreshed Appearance

Blepharoplasty, also known as eyelid surgery, is a cosmetic procedure that removes excess skin, fat, and muscle from the upper or lower eyelids. It helps rejuvenate the eyes by reducing puffiness, sagging, and drooping skin that can make a person look tired or older than they feel.

"Blepharoplasty restores a youthful, natural look to the eyes — bringing brightness back to your expression."

Why People Choose Blepharoplasty

Many individuals choose eyelid surgery to enhance their facial appearance and improve vision obstructed by drooping eyelids. Common reasons include:

  • To remove excess skin that causes eyelid drooping
  • To reduce puffiness or bags under the eyes
  • To improve field of vision affected by sagging eyelids
  • To achieve a more youthful, refreshed appearance

Types of Blepharoplasty Procedures

The procedure can target either the upper or lower eyelids, or both, depending on individual needs:

  • Upper Blepharoplasty: Removes sagging skin and excess tissue from the upper eyelids to open up the eyes.
  • Lower Blepharoplasty: Eliminates under-eye bags and tightens the lower eyelid area.
  • Combined Blepharoplasty: A full rejuvenation procedure addressing both upper and lower eyelids.

Benefits of Blepharoplasty

After recovery, patients typically notice brighter, more youthful eyes and a well-rested appearance. The results are long-lasting and can boost both confidence and comfort by improving vision and facial aesthetics.
